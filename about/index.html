About the 3:17 Interference Archive

The Archive is a fictional civic-research initiative documenting small continuity anomalies associated with the global maintenance window hypothesis. Our tone is intentionally straight-faced: calm language helps separate observation from panic, and panic is notoriously bad for note-taking.

We maintain a public-facing report index, synchronization briefs, and a monitoring dashboard to help readers engage critically with speculative systems language.
