About the 3:17 Counter-Archive

This project is a direct counter-theory response to theylive.site. We preserve its deadpan civic aesthetic while arguing for grounded explanations over hidden-reality maintenance systems.

Our mission is not to mock unusual experiences. It is to improve interpretive rigor: better logs, stronger baselines, and explicit alternatives before extraordinary conclusions.

Think of this as skeptical worldbuilding—same atmosphere, opposite thesis.

Method Commitments

  • Respect observer experience while challenging overconfident interpretation.
  • Separate observed facts from narrative framing.
  • Require confounder checks for high-intensity claims.
  • Prefer explanations with measurable variables.

What This Site Is (and Isn't)

It is a stylized archive, a writing project, and a practical guide to skeptical analysis. It is not a medical, legal, or emergency service.

Bureau of Ordinary Explanations. This site is satirical fiction with a skeptical lens.
