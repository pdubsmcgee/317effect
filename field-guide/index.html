Counter-Method Guide

Use this protocol to evaluate 03:17-style anomalies without defaulting to the patch-window narrative.

Edition 2.0 · Bureau of Ordinary Explanations

Five-Step Check

  1. Capture: write exactly what happened in one sentence.
  2. Context: log sleep, stress, interruptions, and device activity.
  3. Confounders: list at least two ordinary causes.
  4. Compare: check if pattern repeats under similar conditions.
  5. Conclude carefully: choose the narrowest explanation that fits.

Interpretation Traps

  • Timestamp Magnetism: once a time becomes iconic, you notice it more often.
  • Story Borrowing: reading others' reports can shape your own memory framing.
  • Single-Cause Urge: complex days generate weird moments without one master cause.
  • Confidence Creep: retelling events can make uncertain details feel certain.

Cross-Site Comparison Prompt

For any claim from theylive.site, ask: what baseline variable would change this interpretation? If no variable can change it, treat the claim as lore, not evidence.
