FAQ

Is 03:17 always the active patch window?

It is the most commonly reported local marker. Regional drift, travel schedules, and daylight offsets can move activity by several minutes.

What should observers do during active windows?

Remain calm, document neutral facts, and avoid strong conclusions from single events. Sequence order matters more than dramatic wording.

Can this affect daily tasks?

Only subtly in our narrative framework: brief pauses, misplaced objects, and transient recall delays have the highest reported frequency.

How do I submit a usable observation?

Include timestamp, location type, baseline conditions, what you expected to happen, and what occurred instead. Keep it short and specific.
