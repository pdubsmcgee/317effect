Archive Glossary

Canonical definitions used across theory, evidence, and field operations. Keep this page open during cross-page review to reduce terminology drift.

Cognitive Gap Window (CGW)
A short lapse in executive continuity where intent tracking can temporarily fail.
Interstitial Capture Zone (ICZ)
A location or routine context where CGW incidence rises measurably.
Phase Drift Envelope (PDE)
Post-event period where memory confidence and factual accuracy diverge.
Threshold Anchor
A spoken or written pre-commitment made before crossing a known transition point.
Narrative Backfill
Automatic story construction that masks uncertainty after a gap event.
Digital Drift
Unplanned short-form edits or interactions that appear to originate from the same user during low-awareness windows.
Transit Lag Event
A report cluster tied to platform changes, gate crossings, elevator exits, or other route transitions.
