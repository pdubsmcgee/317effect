Text Loop During Routine Email Review

2026-01-16 · 08:42

A single sentence required four reads despite normal alertness and caffeine baseline.

mediumtext-loopreadinglatency

Observer reported semantic lock delay while reading line 2 of a familiar message during a low-distraction work block.

Each pass felt "new" until phrase boundaries were spoken aloud, at which point comprehension resolved immediately.

No visual issues or device glitches were observed. Restarting the email draft did not reproduce the loop.

Suggested note: compare silent reading versus whisper reading during suspected loops, and track whether punctuation marks influence recovery.
